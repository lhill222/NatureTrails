Meet the Animals

Prepare to encounter an astonishing array of wildlife beyond the Big Five. From graceful giraffes delicately grazing on treetops to stealthy cheetahs sprinting across the savannah, from playful hippos basking in waterholes to vibrant flocks of exotic birds adorning the sky, our virtual safari will introduce you to the diverse tapestry of life that flourishes in Africa's grasslands, deserts, and lush rainforests.
